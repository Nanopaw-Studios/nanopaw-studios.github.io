Who We Are

An indie studio
with big ideas.

Nanopaw Studios (formerly Nanodogs Studios) is an independent game development company based in the United Kingdom. Founded in 2023, we're a team of passionate developers driven by a single belief: great games should be for everyone.

We build across genres — from high-velocity action platformers to atmospheric horror and experimental multiplayer survival — always united by a commitment to craft, creativity, and player experience.

"Making games for everyone."
— Our Motto
